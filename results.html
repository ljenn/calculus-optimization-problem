Conclusion: The Results

So now that we know Parker has to travel 62.45 meters to reach her desired location, we can calculate the amount of time for her to get there.


Remember: She travels at a rate of 50 meters per 10 minutes, or 5 meters per minute (wow, slow), and has to rest 1 minute after 50 meters.


Using basic algebra to add this up, this would mean she would take 10 minutes traveling the first 50 meters, 1 minute to rest, and 2 minutes and 29.4 seconds to finish the remaining 12.45 meters.


That's a total of 13 minutes and 29.4 seconds to get to her desired spot. BUT, we would have to double that for the roundtrip, which means travel time is 26 minutes and 58.8 seconds. If she wants to spend 45 minutes at the reef, Parker will either have to learn to swim normally or go for the rental.
